Word Puzzle Rule:

To play the Word Puzzle Game, you must make the longest English word you can from the letters.

For example, you may be given the letters I R E T U C R R E. The answer, the longest word possible is RECRUITER.

Number Puzzle Rule:

To play the Number Puzzle Game, you must get as close as possible to the target number using the basic arithmetic operations and some or all of the smaller numbers.

For example, you may be shown the values 5, 8, 35, 20, 10 and the target number 54. The answer, in this case, is (8 * 35 - 20 + 10) / 5 = 54.
